# Paper Reading

Search for and read one paper that presents an optimization problem. You may choose a paper from any area of knowledge, preferably one related to your PhD or Master research.

**Suggested databases:**

* IEEE and ACM conference and journal databases
* Springer and Elsevier journals
* SBPO (Simpósio Brasileiro de Pesquisa Operacional) conference proceedings
* SBrT (Simpósio Brasileiro de Telecomunicações e Processamento de Sinais) conference proceedings
* SBRC (Simpósio Brasileiro de Redes de Computadores e Sistemas Distribuídos) conference proceedings
* Other relevant scientific databases

It is important that the paper has been formally published (please avoid preprints) and that it clearly presents the problem formulation and the mathematical model, as discussed in class. Also verify whether the paper classifies the problem according to the categories studied in class.

Submit the PDF of the selected paper via SIGAA. Please read your chosen paper carefully, as some of the selected papers will be discussed in class. If your paper is selected, you will be asked to present and explain the problem formulation and solution approach to the class.
